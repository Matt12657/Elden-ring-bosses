Brick Hammer

Brick Hammer is a Great Hammer (Warhammer) in Elden Ring. The Brick Hammer scales primarily with Strength and is a good Weapon for high strength players who can hold this weapon's weight. The weight is worth the ability to crush enemy armor, sometimes along with the enemy itself.
