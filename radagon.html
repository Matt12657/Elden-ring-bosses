ELDEN RING

Radagon of the Golden Order

  • Health: NG (13339), NG+ (13353), NG++ (14688), NG+3 (15356), NG+4 (16023), NG+5 (17359), NG+6 (18026), NG+7 (18694)
  • Deals Standard Damage, Slash Damage, Strike Damage, and Holy Damage.
  • Can block spells and throwable items with very high damage reduction (all damage types). Reduces status buildup as well.
  • He can use his "shield arm" to block melee attacks as well, but this will happen only on accident (if Radagon is attacked during blocking a range attack).
  • Parriable: Yes (Regular, non-charged melee attacks only. It also only provides an opportunity for riposte on every 3rd parry)
  • Can be poise broken. Poise: 120
  • Particularly affected by offensive Godskin Incanatations
  • Upon defeat, a cutscene will play and the fight against the Elden Beast will begin.
